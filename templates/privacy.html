Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic details such as your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we also store the expense records you create, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to operate and improve Spendly. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries and category breakdowns, and authenticating your account. We do not use your financial data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secured servers. We apply reasonable technical measures — including password hashing and encrypted connections — to protect your information from unauthorized access. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take your privacy seriously and continuously work to protect your data.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting and infrastructure. These providers are contractually bound to handle your data only as necessary to support the operation of this application. We do not integrate with advertising networks or analytics platforms that track you across other sites.

5. Your Rights and Data Control

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data associated with your account at any time. You may delete your account from your profile settings, which will permanently remove all your expense data from our system. For any data requests, contact us directly.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Spendly uses a session cookie solely to keep you logged in between page visits. We do not use tracking cookies, fingerprinting, or any cross-site analytics. You can disable cookies in your browser, but doing so will prevent you from staying signed in.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Spendly after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
